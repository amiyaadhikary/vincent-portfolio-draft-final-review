Recent Research Articles

Development of predictive model for predicting postoperative BMI and optimize bariatric surgery: a single center pilot study

The pilot study addresses the challenge of predicting postoperative outcomes, particularly body mass index (BMI) trajectories, following bariatric surgery.

Published by Vincent Ochs, Anja Tobler, Julia Wolleb and more...

Publication date: December 1, 2024

Machine learning-based preoperative analytics for the prediction of anastomotic leakage in colorectal surgery: a swiss pilot study

Anastomotic leakage (AL), a severe complication following colorectal surgery, arises from defects at the anastomosis site. This study evaluates the feasibility of predicting AL using machine learning (ML) algorithms based on preoperative data.

Published by Vincent Ochs, Stephanie Taha-Mehlitz, Larissa Wentzler and more...

Publication date: June 22, 2024

Development and validation of a predictive model of the hospital cost associated with bariatric surgery

This study explores the use of Machine Learning, specifically the Random Forest model, to predict care costs for bariatric and metabolic surgery, achieving a 12.7% mean absolute percentage error. The results demonstrate its potential for improving hospital cost analysis and operational efficiency, though further research is needed for multi-center validation.

Published by Vincent Ochs, Stephanie Taha-Mehlitz, Larissa Wentzler and more...

Publication date: November 1, 2023
